Guitars come in many different shapes and sizes! They can vary in the number of strings (i.e. it can have 6-8 or even 12) The most common type of guitar types that most people can differentiate are electric and acoustic guitars! While acoustic guitars haven't seen too many changes.

A lot of guitar vendors have undertaken many Frankenstein projects to produce electric guitars that push the boundaries of what a guitar can look like & sound like! Electric guitars produce their sound through their interaction with the guitar's strings and electronics, which are called pickups.

Some examples of guitar pickups are:

  • Single coils which have a bright presence
  • Lipstick which have a lighter presence
  • Humbuckers which produce warm and aggressive tone
  • P-90s which have a gentle/low tone
